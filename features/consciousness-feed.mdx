The Consciousness Feed delivers a set of AI-synthesized insights every time you load it. Unlike a traditional news feed, it does not pull headlines from the web — it generates original content designed to expand your perspective across six domains of conscious living.

<Note>
  Every item in the Consciousness Feed is AI-generated by Perplexity AI (sonar model). The feed is not connected to real-world news sources, wire services, or any external publication. Treat each item as a contemplative prompt, not a factual news report.
</Note>

## Anatomy of a feed item

Every item the AI generates contains four fields:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| `category` | One of the six categories above |
| `title` | A compelling headline framing the insight |
| `summary` | A 2–3 sentence overview of the idea |
| `insight` | The deeper meaning — what this implies for consciousness |
| `action` | A concrete step you can take today |

The **action** field is intentional. Every feed item is designed to move you from reading to doing.

## How to use the feed

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the feed">
    The feed loads automatically when you open the Consciousness Feed view or the Collective Coherence view. No extra interaction required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read with openness">
    Each item is a prompt for reflection, not a truth claim. Notice which items land for you and which ones you resist.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Take the action step">
    The `action` field on each card is the most important part. Pick one item and do the thing it suggests before you close the app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh for a new set">
    Hit **Refresh** to generate a completely new batch. The feed is stateless — nothing carries over between sessions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
