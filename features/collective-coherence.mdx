Collective Coherence shows you a live count of people currently in a coherent state and lets you understand how your own practice contributes to a shared global field. You are not just using an app — you are participating in something larger.

## What you see

When you open the Collective Coherence view, three metrics are displayed:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Global Coherence" icon="globe">
    The running total of people currently in a coherent state. This number updates automatically every 5 seconds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Active Participants" icon="users">
    The number of people actively exploring the app right now. This also fluctuates every 5 seconds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Collective Field" icon="heart">
    A qualitative signal — always "Strengthening" — reminding you that your presence contributes to everyone else.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  The Global Coherence and Active Participants numbers are simulated on the client side and updated every 5 seconds via a local interval. They represent the concept of collective participation rather than real-time telemetry from other users.
</Note>

## The Consciousness Feed

Below the metrics, Collective Coherence embeds the full [Consciousness Feed](/features/consciousness-feed). The feed loads automatically when you open the view and surfaces six AI-generated insights across science, movement, wisdom, transformation, environment, and technology.

You can hit **Refresh** at any time to generate a fresh set of insights.

## How your presence contributes

Resonance's broader mission is to support a global shift in consciousness. The Collective Coherence feature makes that abstract idea concrete:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app with intention">
    Simply arriving with awareness — rather than scrolling out of habit — is itself a coherent act.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Engage with the feed">
    Reading an insight, sitting with a question, or taking an action step from the feed is a practice, not passive consumption.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return to the world">
    The point is never to stay in the app. Every coherent moment you bring back into your day strengthens the field for everyone.
  </Step>
</Steps>
