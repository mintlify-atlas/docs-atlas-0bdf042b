Presence Protocols is built on a single, counterintuitive idea: the most conscious thing you can do is close the app. This feature gives you a daily intention to carry into the world, then actively encourages you to go live it.

> Sometimes the most conscious act is closing the app. Generate a daily intention, then go live it. This is where the real work happens.

## Generating a daily intention

Tap **Generate Daily Intention** to request a fresh intention from the AI. Each intention contains four parts:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Core intention" icon="heart">
    A single theme or phrase to hold throughout the day. Displayed prominently as the anchor for everything else.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Morning practice" icon="sun">
    A 3–5 minute practice to begin your day. Grounded, specific, and completable before you check your phone.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Midday check-in" icon="clock">
    A prompt to pause at midday and reconnect with your intention. Usually a question to ask yourself.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Evening reflection" icon="moon">
    A question to sit with before sleep that helps you close the day with awareness.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Generations are stateless — each tap produces a brand-new intention. Your previous intentions are not saved unless you copy them manually or write them in [Notes](/features/notes).

## The Digital Sabbath

After your intention is generated, a **Digital Sabbath** timer appears below the three time-based practices.

The Digital Sabbath is intentional time away from the app — and from screens altogether. The concept is simple:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read your intention">
    Take a moment with the core intention and the three practices. Let them settle.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start the Digital Sabbath">
    Tap **Start Digital Sabbath**. The timer begins counting up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Put the phone down">
    Close the app. Step away from screens. Be with your intention in the physical world.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return when ready">
    When you come back, tap **End Session** to see how long you were away. The app doesn't push notifications to interrupt you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

While the session is active, the app displays: *"You're present. No need to check back. Just be."*

<Tip>
  You do not need to watch the timer. Start it, put your device face-down, and check the elapsed time when you naturally return. The timer is for your own reflection — not a metric that goes anywhere.
</Tip>

## The philosophy

Resonance is designed to send you back to your life, more awake. Presence Protocols is the most direct expression of that design. The app is not trying to maximize your time in it — it is trying to maximize the quality of your time outside it.

<Info>
  Intentions are generated by Perplexity AI (sonar model) via the `/api/intention` endpoint. Only the generation request is sent — no personal data, journal text, or context from other features is included. See [Radical Transparency](/features/radical-transparency) for the full picture.
</Info>
