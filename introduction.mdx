## What is Resonance?

Resonance is a full-stack web application built with React 18, TypeScript, and an Express.js backend. It uses AI to reflect your inner world back to you — your emotional patterns, blind spots, flow states, and growth edges — so you can live with greater clarity and intention.

Every feature is powered by Perplexity's `sonar` model through a rate-limited Express proxy. The proxy keeps your API key secure and ensures the AI never has direct access to your device.

<Note>
  Resonance is open source. You can inspect every line of code, understand every design decision, and contribute improvements at [github.com/neuralboy/reasonance-app](https://github.com/neuralboy/reasonance-app).
</Note>

## The philosophy

Resonance is built around a single north star:

> *"We succeed when you trust yourself more, not the AI more."*

This isn't an app designed to create dependency. The AI doesn't know you. It can't feel your pain, your joy, or your becoming. What it can do is pattern-match at scale — and sometimes, seeing your own patterns reflected back is enough to recognize what was already there.

Every feature is designed to send you back to your life: more awake, more aligned, more alive. The intelligence isn't in the algorithm. It's in you.

## AI as a mirror, not a replacement

Most AI products are built to make themselves indispensable. Resonance is built to make itself unnecessary.

When you write a journal entry in Consciousness Mapping, the AI doesn't tell you who to be. It shows you who you already are — your current emotional frequency, the edges where you're ready to grow, and the triggers that bring you into presence. The insight belongs to you. The AI is just the mirror.

This principle runs through every feature:

- **Shadow Integration** doesn't diagnose you — it gently names what you might already sense.
- **Ancient Wisdom** doesn't prescribe — it offers a teaching from a living tradition and lets you decide what fits.
- **Presence Protocols** doesn't keep you engaged — it actively reminds you to close the app and go live.

## What makes Resonance different

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Radical transparency" icon="eye">
    You can see exactly how the AI works: what model is used, what data is sent, what is stored, and what is never collected. No black boxes, no hidden agendas.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Presence-first design" icon="heart">
    The Presence Protocols feature exists specifically to remind you to step away from the screen. A good tool knows when to get out of the way.
  </Card>
  <Card title="No data selling" icon="shield">
    Resonance does not sell your data, train models on your inputs, use tracking cookies, or share data with third parties beyond the AI API call needed to process your request.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Session-only storage" icon="clock">
    Your consciousness mapping sessions, synchronicity results, and shadow work live only in your browser. Refresh the page and they're gone. Nothing is persisted server-side without your explicit action.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
  The only exception to session-only storage is the Notes feature, which uses Supabase to persist your saved reflections. You own those entries. Resonance does not analyze or sell them.
</Info>
